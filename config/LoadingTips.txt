## MODPACK
Type 'discord' in the terminal to suggest something for the modpack.
Found a bug? Type 'discord' in the terminal to report it in discord server.
Enjoying the modpack? Type 'modpack' in the terminal and smash that like button!
Having a soft-lock? Press CTRL+ALT+SHIFT+Q to return to the main menu.
## GAMEPLAY
Approaching large water areas could be very dangerous.
Fire exit may be blocked by planks, preventing entry or exit. They can be broken with any weapon, such as a shovel.
Found a bug? Then kill it with the shovel.
Use the arrow keys to switch between player cameras in the terminal.
Enabled boombox makes Hygroderes move faster and not dealing damage.
Stand on railings to avoid Hygroderes.
Players can sprint jump over a Hygrodere if there is enough space.
Hygroderes can be killed with Zap-gun.
Damaging a Hygrodere will slightly increase its speed for a short time.
Terminal home page contains a lot of useful commands.
After killing a Masked enemy, you can grab the mask off the dead body and sell it.
Pulling out the apparatus will turn off all lights inside and outside.
Once the apparatus is pulled out, all players in the facility will start to take damage every hour.
Locked doors can be destroyed using shotgun, any melee weapon, turret fire, or landmines.
Security doors can be opened using terminal, Zap-gun, or during a power outage.
Press [ M ] to mute yourself.
Use Zap-gun to temporarily disable landmines.
Type 'refund' in the terminal to cancel a dropship order and receive your money back before it lands.
Circuit bees will attack some enemies that gets too close to them.
Circuit bees have a chance to set Forest Keepers on fire.
Earth Leviathans hunt nearby enemies, but they ignore smaller prey.
Lobby automatically re-opens when going back to orbit.
Flashlights dims and flickers when reaching 30% battery
Gifts sometimes can be lethal
Bracken drags grabbed players doing gradual damage toward a favorite spot
Having an level 2 visor crack increases oxygen consumption
Dig a snow on snowy weather to clear paths
During Flooded and rarely Stormy and Rainy weather water appears indoors and level increases slowly
## QUOTA
Credits will be reduced by 5% for each dead player without recovering their body
Quota will be increased by 3% for 2+ dead player without recovering their body
Quota multiplies for each new player in the lobby if there are more than 2
